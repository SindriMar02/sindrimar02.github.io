About

Independent. Accountable.

ARTIX ehf. is an independent, Iceland-based defence and security supplier and integrator, based at Smiðjuvellir 17, 300 Akranes, on Faxaflói.

An independent supplier — close to the manufacturers, close to the end-user.

ARTIX closes the distance between the manufacturers who build defence and security equipment and the end-users who depend on it. We source it, vet it, specify it, and stand behind it through life.

We are not a manufacturer and we are not an operator. We are the accountable line between the two — local presence on Faxaflói, regional knowledge, and a single point of contact from first enquiry to long-term support.
