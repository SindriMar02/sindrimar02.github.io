Source to support

Six steps, one accountable line.

One continuous responsibility from first enquiry to long-term support — never handed off.

  1. 01

    Source

    An established network of trusted international manufacturers across the domains our end-users require.

  2. 02

    Vet

    Every manufacturer assessed for fit, compliance and the ability to support equipment through its service life.

  3. 03

    Match

    The right capability specified for the mission and the environment — never more than the task requires.

  4. 04

    Deliver

    Procurement, logistics and export managed, so equipment arrives ready and accounted for.

  5. 05

    Integrate

    Equipment brought into the end-user's environment — supplied and supported, never operated by us.

  6. 06

    Sustain

    Training, spares and through-life support that keep capability ready long after delivery.
